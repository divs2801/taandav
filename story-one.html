Unit 1 – Origin of Natya

Long ago, when people struggled with good and bad choices, the gods were worried. They asked Lord Brahma for a way to teach everyone important life lessons in a fun way. Brahma decided to create something new—Natya Veda, a mix of speech, music, expression, and emotion from the four Vedas, combining dance, drama, and music into one.
