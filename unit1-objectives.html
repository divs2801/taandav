Unit 1 - Origin of Natya

Objectives:

  • Understand the divine origins of Natya as explained in Bharata’s Natya Sastra.
  • Learn how Lord Brahma created Natya Veda by integrating elements from the four Vedas.
  • Explore how Bharata was taught Natya Veda and trained his disciples to perform plays.
  • Study the significance of the first production, Asura Parajaya, and its impact on the demons.
  • Discover the role of Viswakarma in constructing the Natya Griha to protect performances.
  • Recognize Natya’s sacred and instructional role in guiding individuals toward moral and righteous living.
