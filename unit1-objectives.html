Unit 1 – Origins of Natya

Objectives:

  • Understand the divine origins of Natya as explained in Bharata’s Natya Sastra.
  • Learn how Lord Brahma created Natya Veda by integrating elements from the four Vedas.
  • Recognize Natya’s sacred and instructional role in guiding individuals toward moral and righteous living.
